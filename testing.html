Our Programs

Comprehensive skating programs designed for every skill level and aspiration.

Learn to Skate

Perfect for beginners, our foundational program teaches basic skating skills in a fun, supportive environment.

  • Basic balance and movement
  • Forward and backward skating
  • Stopping techniques
  • Safety fundamentals

Competitive Training

Advanced training for serious skaters preparing for competitions and pursuing skating excellence.

  • Advanced technique development
  • Competition preparation
  • Performance coaching
  • Mental training

Adult Classes

Specialized programs for adult learners who want to experience the joy of ice skating at any age.

  • Adult-focused instruction
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Fitness and recreation
  • Social skating opportunities
