Where Champions Are Made

Located in Egna, Italy, Young Goose Academy (YGA) is a top-tier skating center based at Würth Arena, offering high-level training and integrated academics to over 70 international athletes.

Location & Facilities

Based in the state-of-the-art Würth Arena, YGA was inspired by Egna’s local skating heritage and now provides professional coaching alongside academic support for athletes.

Programs & Coaching

Offers training in singles, ice dance, pairs, and synchro. Led by experienced coaches including Matteo Zanni and supported by an international staff.

Top Skaters

Athletes like Naoki Rossi, Daniel Grassl, and Gabriele Frangipani have trained here, making YGA a destination for competitive excellence.

Global Impact

YGA welcomes skaters from around the world for year-round training, seasonal camps, and international collaborations in figure skating development.
